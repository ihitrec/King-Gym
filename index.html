About us

We are a newly opened gym with high quality equipment in three Dublin locations. You are welcome regardless of skill level. Our dedicated team will be there to help you with anything you need. There also is an option to receive a more personalised nutritional and workout plan from our expert personal trainers. Your membership includes access to any of our gyms. Come visit our Merrion Square gym which is our biggest location with a swimming pool. It's a great way to relax after a workout. If you are unsure, just book a free training and see how you like our gym experience.
